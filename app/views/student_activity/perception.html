Techno-Cultural Event PERCEPTION:
The institute organises an intercollegiate techno-cultural activity called “PERCEPTION”. This event hosts various technical competitions such as C programming, Circuit design, Robotics, Paper presentation etc. It also hosts inter-college cultural competitions in singing, dancing, etc. More than 700 students from Maharashtra state participate in this event.

The students perform cultural programmes and hold various sports competitions. EHSAAS, a social endeavour has been initiated by our institute in which our students invite orphans, down-trodden children and mentally retarded children and teach and entertain them. Those children also perform cultural programmes. Sugam Sandhya, an evening devoted to Marathi songs is well appreciated and enjoyed by staff and students.
