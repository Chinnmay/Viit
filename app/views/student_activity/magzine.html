Lighthouse


Lighthouse is VIIT'S annual magazine. it is s mediumm through which budding writers, poets and artists can showcase their talent. The theme, design and concept are all decided by students with the guidance of a few teacher co-ordinators. it goes beyond academics, by encouraging students to be creative with their content.

It represents a mixture of thoughts and emotions all poured on paper and showcased for the entire VIIT community to see and appreciate.
