Student registration for various Co-curricular and Extra-Curricular Activities for the year 2016-17

VIIT student's conduct various Co-curricular and Extra-Curricular Activities throughout the year. These activities are categorized into following groups and faculty members are appointed as In-charge of different activates. Any student, who wishes to participate in any of the activities mentioned below, must register for it. Students can give 3 preferences and he/she will be allowed to participate in one activity. Students, who do not register, will not be allowed to participate.
